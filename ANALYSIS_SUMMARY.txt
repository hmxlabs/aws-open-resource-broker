RATIONALE:

================================================================================
THE GOLDEN RULE
================================================================================

"Would a business analyst care about this event?"
  
  YES → Domain Event (domain/base/events/)
        Examples: RequestCreated, MachineProvisioned, TemplateValidated
        
  NO  → Infrastructure Event (infrastructure/events/)
        Examples: RepositoryOperationStarted, SlowQueryDetected

This simple test prevents future misclassification.
